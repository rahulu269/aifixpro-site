Causal Impact Analysis

Understand the true impact of your interventions. Our Causal Impact Analysis helps you precisely measure the effect of marketing campaigns, policy changes, or product launches, isolating them from other factors.

Upload Your Data for Analysis

To begin your Causal Impact analysis, please upload your data file (CSV or Excel) using the secure Google Form below. Ensure your file is formatted correctly with your time series data and intervention points clearly marked.

Accessing Your Results:

Once you've submitted your data, our AI will begin processing your Causal Impact analysis. This may take a few minutes depending on the complexity and size of your data.

We will email the results to the address you provided in the form. Additionally, you can check our Public Results Archive (coming soon) for a list of recent analyses. Please use the unique ID provided after your form submission to locate your specific report.

For any questions, please contact us.
